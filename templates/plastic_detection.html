1. Drone Concept

Automated drones are deployed to remove ocean plastics. They are effective for targeting small particles.

  • Advantages: Inexpensive units, unmanned, flexible deployment.
  • Disadvantages: Long cleanup time, potential for by-catch, high operating cost, impractical for large debris.

2. Vessel-Based Concept

This ship-based solution leverages existing technologies for plastic collection in marine environments.

  • Advantages: Low capital costs.
  • Disadvantages: Long cleanup times, emissions, high costs, low efficiency, and challenges with small particles.

3. Ocean Cleanup Array

This concept uses floating barriers and platforms moored to the seabed to catch buoyant plastic particles, allowing marine organisms to pass underneath.

Invented by Boyan Slat in 2013.
