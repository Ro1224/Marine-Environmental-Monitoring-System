Contact the Indian Coast Guard

The Indian Coast Guard is the central authority for oil spill management in India. The Coast Guard is responsible for coordinating the response to oil spills in India's maritime zones.

Here are some ways to contact the Indian Coast Guard for oil spills:

Contact Details

National Centre: +91-11-23384934, dte-ops@indiancoastguard.nic.in

Western Region: +91-022-24371932, rhq-west@indiancoastguard.nic.in

Eastern Region: +91-44-25391718, ops-east@indiancoastguard.nic.in

North West Region: +91-79-23243315, rhq-nw@indiancoastguard.nic.in

Andaman & Nicobar Islands: +91-3192-231638, rhq-an@indiancoastguard.nic.in

The Indian Coast Guard has developed the National Oil Spill Disaster Contingency Plan (NOSDCP) to respond to oil spills in Indian waters. The NOSDCP divides India's maritime zones into three regions, each with a Regional Commander and a Response Centre.
